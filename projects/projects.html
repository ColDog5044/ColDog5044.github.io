DJ ColDog

DJ ColDog started off producing music on December 25th, 2018.

Phase 1

  • Phase 1 consists of making music off of templates. The Garageband DAW was used.

Phase 2

  • Phase 2 started with City Lights.
  • Phase 2 consists of exploring Garageband samples and putting them togther to make his own music.

Phase 3

  • Phase 3 started of the Highs and Lows Album released on SoundCloud.
  • Phase 3 consists of custom drum beats.

Phase 4

  • Phase 4 started with the idea of Life in January 2020.
  • Phase 4 marked the start of DJ ColDog's rapping career.

Phase 5

  • Phase 5 is the start of The First Dog, set to release in the Summer of 2022.
    • The First Dog consists of two features, Speedy C and Trevdog, some fresh cooked beats, and tasty bars.
